Our Mission

At PhishGuard, we're on a mission to make cybersecurity accessible to everyone, especially girls and young women who are often underserved in the tech security space. We believe that learning about online safety should be empowering, not intimidating.

Through our adorable teddy bear and panda mascots, we transform complex security concepts into friendly, approachable lessons. We combine cutting-edge threat detection with gamified learning to create an experience that's both educational and delightful.
